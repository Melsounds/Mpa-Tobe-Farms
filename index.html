About Our Farm

Green Valley Farm is more than just a business — it's a way of life rooted in respect for the land and a commitment to feeding our community with honesty and integrity.

🌱 Our Story

What started as a small 20-acre plot in 1998 has grown into a thriving 500-acre mixed farm. Founded by John and Mary Thompson, the farm has been passed down through three generations, each adding their own innovations while staying true to sustainable, organic farming principles.

🌍 Our Mission

To produce high-quality, nutritious food while preserving the environment for future generations. We believe that farming should work with nature, not against it. Every decision we make considers the impact on soil health, water conservation, and biodiversity.

🚜 Our Methods

We use crop rotation, composting, and natural pest control to maintain soil fertility without synthetic chemicals. Our livestock are raised on open pastures with access to clean water and natural feed. No hormones, no antibiotics — just nature's way.

👨‍👩‍👧‍👦 Our Community

We employ over 30 local workers and partner with nearby schools and food banks. Every Saturday, we host a farmers market where neighbors gather to buy fresh produce, share recipes, and connect. We believe strong communities grow from shared meals.
